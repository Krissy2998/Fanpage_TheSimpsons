About.

Thanks for stopping by!

I am a budding developer who ensure she finds time to keeps her inner child alive and thriving.

This is acomplished by Rollerskating, Reading comic booked and of course, watching cartoons.

I hope you will enjoy one of my all time favourite cartoon family, The Simpsons

My love for The Simpsons stems back over 20 years when I moved to the UK from the Caribbean and I found such a comfort and calm by having The Simpson on telly every evening.

This was a time long before the many streaming and on demand services we have at our disposal.

Over the years I have learnt, laughed and enjoyed the many adventures and misadventures they have experienced, therefore I chose dedicate my project to a part of my childhood that I intend to enjoy for many more years to come.
